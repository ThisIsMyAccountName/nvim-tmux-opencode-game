## Commands For This Task
- `:e <path_to_file>` - open another file.
- `:vsplit <path_to_file>` - compare files side-by-side.
- `<C-w>w` - switch between split windows.
- `:%s/<lookup>/<replace>/g` - replace lookup with replace in whole file; `%` means whole file, `g` means all matches per line.
- `ciw` / `cw` - replace current word / from cursor to end of word.
